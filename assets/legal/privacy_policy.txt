PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: March 31, 2026

1. INTRODUCTION

Silent Morse ("we," "our," or "the App") respects your privacy. This policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

• Account information: When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name, email (if provided), and a unique identifier.
• Messages: The content of messages you send and receive is stored in Firebase to deliver the service.
• Device token: We store your Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token to deliver push notifications.
• User reports: If you report another user or content, the report details are stored for moderation purposes.

3. ADVERTISING

Silent Morse displays ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. On iOS, we request your permission via App Tracking Transparency (ATT) before any tracking occurs. You may decline and still use the app normally.

You can remove all ads permanently through an in-app purchase.

4. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

• To provide and maintain the messaging service
• To authenticate your identity
• To allow other users to find you (by username or display name)
• To display advertisements (via Google AdMob)
• To deliver push notifications for incoming messages
• To moderate content and enforce our Terms of Service
• To comply with legal obligations

5. DATA STORAGE

Your data is stored on Firebase (Google Cloud) servers. Conversations are automatically pruned to keep them lightweight — Silent Morse is not designed for long-term message storage. You may delete your account at any time through the App settings, which removes your account data from our systems.

6. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

• Firebase (Google LLC) — authentication, database, cloud functions, push notifications
• Google AdMob (Google LLC) — advertising
• Google Sign-In / Apple Sign-In — authentication handled by these providers

These services are governed by their own privacy policies. See https://policies.google.com/privacy for Google services.

7. DATA SHARING

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data:
• With other users as necessary for the messaging feature (e.g., your display name, messages)
• With service providers (Firebase, AdMob) who help us operate the App
• When required by law or to protect our rights

8. SECURITY

We use industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
• Access your personal data
• Correct inaccurate data
• Delete your data
• Export your data
• Opt out of certain processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at il_shapiro@hotmail.com or delete your account through the App settings.

10. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

The App is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

11. CHANGES

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

12. CONTACT

For privacy questions, contact us at il_shapiro@hotmail.com.
