1. INTRODUCTION

Silent Morse ("we," "our," or "the App") respects your privacy. This policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

• Account information: When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name, email (if provided), and a unique identifier.
• Messages: The content of messages you send and receive is stored to deliver the service.
• Device information: We may collect device type and app version for troubleshooting.
• Usage data: We may collect how you interact with the App to improve the service.

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

• To provide and maintain the messaging service
• To authenticate your identity
• To allow other users to find you (by username or display name)
• To improve the App and fix issues
• To comply with legal obligations

4. DATA STORAGE

Your data is stored on Firebase (Google Cloud) servers. Messages are stored until you or the recipient deletes them, or you delete your account.

5. DATA SHARING

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data:
• With other users as necessary for the messaging feature (e.g., your display name, messages)
• With service providers (e.g., Firebase) who help us operate the App
• When required by law or to protect our rights

6. SECURITY

We use industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
• Access your personal data
• Correct inaccurate data
• Delete your data
• Export your data
• Opt out of certain processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at [YOUR_EMAIL] or delete your account through the App.

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

The App is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
