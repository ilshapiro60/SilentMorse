Privacy Policy

App: Silent Morse
Developer: Ilya Shapiro
Effective date: March 28, 2026

Overview

Silent Morse is a haptic Morse code messenger. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app handles and how it is managed.

Data We Do Not Collect

Silent Morse does not collect, store, sell, or share any personal information. We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking of any kind. No personally identifiable information ever leaves your device to our servers.

Message Data

Messages are transmitted in real time over a secure Firebase connection and are used solely to deliver haptic vibration patterns to your contact. We do not log, archive, or store message content on our servers.

On-device, a rolling buffer of no more than 10 recent messages per connection is kept in local storage solely to render the current session on screen. This buffer is not backed up, not synced to any cloud, and is cleared automatically as new messages arrive. No message history is ever persisted beyond this limit.

Account and Authentication

Silent Morse supports sign-in via Google or Apple. Authentication is handled entirely by those providers using their own privacy policies. We only receive a unique user identifier (UID) to identify your account within Firebase - no name, email address, or profile data is stored by us.

In-App Purchases

Purchase transactions are processed exclusively by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store any payment or billing information.

Third-Party Services

Silent Morse uses Firebase (Google LLC) as its backend infrastructure for real-time messaging and authentication. Firebase's use of data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy

Children's Privacy

Silent Morse is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
