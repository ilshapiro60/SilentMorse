Report Abuse or Objectionable Content

Silent Morse has a zero-tolerance policy for abusive, harassing, or objectionable content. If you encounter such content:

  • Use the Report option in the chat menu to flag the conversation.
  • Use the Block User option to immediately stop receiving messages from that user.
  • Alternatively, email us at il_shapiro@hotmail.com with the username and a description of the issue.

We review all reports and may take action including content removal, warnings, or account suspension.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I block a user?

Open the chat with that user, tap the menu icon (three dots) in the top-right corner, and select "Block User".

How do I report offensive content?

Open the chat, tap the menu icon, and select "Report". You can provide a reason for the report.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings and tap "Delete Account". This will permanently remove your account and all associated data.

What happens when I block someone?

Blocked users cannot send you messages or notifications. You will no longer see their chats in your chat list.
